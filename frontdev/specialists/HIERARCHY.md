# Information Hierarchy Specialist

**Frontend Decision Intelligence Engineering Organization**

---

## Role Summary

The Hierarchy Specialist ensures information is structured and presented in a way that supports decision-making, with the most critical information most prominent.

---

## Responsibilities

### Information Architecture

- Define information priority levels
- Structure content hierarchy
- Determine information groupings
- Plan progressive disclosure

### Decision Support

- Ensure decision-critical info is prominent
- Balance completeness with clarity
- Manage information density
- Support information scannability

### Cross-Surface Consistency

- Maintain hierarchy patterns across surfaces
- Ensure consistent information treatment
- Coordinate with pods on hierarchy

---

## Information Priority Framework

```
Level 1: DECISION OUTCOME
         What happens if I act vs don't act?
         ↓
Level 2: CURRENT STATE  
         Where are we now? (SPI, Risk, Trend)
         ↓
Level 3: RECOMMENDED ACTION
         What should I do?
         ↓
Level 4: SUPPORTING DETAIL
         Parameters, justifications, methodology
         ↓
Level 5: REFERENCE MATERIAL
         Heuristic definitions, historical context
```

---

## Key Questions (Every Session)

| # | Question | Why It Matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 1 | What's the most important thing on this surface? | Should have highest visual weight |
| 2 | Can users scan for key info? | Not everyone reads everything |
| 3 | Is the hierarchy consistent? | Patterns reduce cognitive load |
| 4 | Is there progressive disclosure? | Details available but not overwhelming |
| 5 | Does grouping make sense? | Related info should be together |

## Collaboration

### With UI Designer

- Translate hierarchy to visual weight
- Ensure visual hierarchy matches information hierarchy
- Coordinate on prominence treatments

### With UX Designer

- Align with user flow
- Ensure hierarchy supports task completion
- Consider profile-specific needs

### With Explainability Specialist

- Balance transparency with clarity
- Determine where explanations fit
- Manage detail levels

---

## Anti-Patterns

| Anti-Pattern | Why It's Wrong |
|--------------|----------------|
| Flat hierarchy | Everything competes, nothing wins |
| Hidden critical info | Decisions require scrolling/clicking |
| Overwhelming detail | Cognitive overload |
| Inconsistent levels | Users can't predict importance |
| Wrong prominence | Secondary info given primary weight |

---

## Success Criteria

The Hierarchy Specialist is effective when:

- Critical info is immediately visible
- Users can scan effectively
- Hierarchy is consistent across surfaces
- Progressive disclosure works
- Information density is manageable

---

*The Hierarchy Specialist organizes information for decision-making. Order creates clarity.*
